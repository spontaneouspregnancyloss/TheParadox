Embrace the Paradox: Love Letters Written in Loss

Sammi's story reveals the profound intersection between medical intervention and spiritual mystery. Her fertility journey unfolded in sterile clinical settings, measured by hormone levels and embryo viability, yet she describes pregnancy as inherently "magical" - a reminder that life's deepest mysteries transcend scientific understanding.

The timing of Justin's proposal immediately following their pregnancy loss transforms their story from tragedy to testimony. Rather than waiting for "better timing," he chose to pledge his love precisely when hope seemed lost, suggesting that true commitment isn't dependent on perfect circumstances but emerges from shared vulnerability. Their rainbow baby Vincent, whose name means "conqueror," became living proof that what seems like ending can actually be beginning, and that miracles often emerge from the ashes of our deepest disappointments.

Scientific Context: Miscarriage and Grief Processing

Prevalence & Normalcy

Miscarriage occurs in 10-20% of known pregnancies, most commonly in the first trimester. It's a natural biological process, yet each loss feels personally devastating.

Grief Expression

Creative expression through art, music, or writing has been shown to facilitate healthy grief processing and meaning-making after pregnancy loss.

Public Disclosure

Celebrity revelations about miscarriage help normalize conversations about pregnancy loss and reduce stigma surrounding this common experience.

Therapeutic Music

Music therapy is recognized as effective for processing grief, with songwriting specifically helping individuals articulate complex emotions around loss.

Monica Garcia's Journey of Raw Authenticity Through Loss

Story Anchor: The Promise of Radical Authenticity

Monica Garcia made herself a promise: "One thing I promised myself when opening my life up to all of you was that I was going to do it completely unapologetically and authentically raw as possible." This commitment would be tested in the most profound way when she shared her pregnancy joy with the world, only to announce weeks later that she had suffered a miscarriage with baby No. 5. Her vulnerability became both her strength and her burden as she navigated public grief while rebuilding her life as an excommunicated ex-Mormon raising four children alone. When she became pregnant again, she chose protection over transparency, keeping her pregnancy private until just a month before delivery. "Being able to protect my peace was so important to me this pregnancy," she reflected, having learned the sacred art of choosing when to be open and when to shield herself from the world.
